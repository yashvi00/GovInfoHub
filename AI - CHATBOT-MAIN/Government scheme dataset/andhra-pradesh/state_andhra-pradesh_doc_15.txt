Andhra Pradesh govt. has started AP Dr. YSR Kanti Velugu Scheme Website at drysrkv.ap.gov.in. Under this Kanti Velugu Programme, the state govt. will ensure comprehensive and sustainable Universal Eye Care to all the people. Check AP YSR Kanti Velugu Yojna guidelines, official website, preliminary screening data sheet and complete details.
Dr. YSR Kanti Velugu Programme in AP will organize preliminary screening of all school children in Phase 1. Currently, there are 62,459 schools having 70.41 lakh children. AP govt. will identify children with defective vision and the screening team will include 1 public health staff and 1 asha worker. Each screening team will cover 200 to 250 students every day.
Preliminary screening is to be carried out from 10 Oct to 16 October 2019 and data sheets would be prepared. These Preliminary Screening Data Sheets would be handed over to ANMs who will upload this data at PHCs.
