
Andhra Pradesh govt. will launch a new pilot program Bala Sanjeevani Scheme 2018. Under this scheme, govt. will provide nutritious and high protein food to pregnant women, mothers and the children below 6 years of age. This scheme will benefit nearly 20,000 pregnant women and 5900 children in the district. AP CM Chandrababu Naidu will launch this scheme at Grama Darsini Programme to be held at Gudivada village in S Rayavaram Mandal in Vishakapatnam district.
The primary objective is to reduce pregnant women and children mortality rate and to eradicate malnutrition from the state. After the successful implementation of AP Bala Sanjeevani pilot project, govt. will further expand the scope of this scheme. 
District administration has made elaborate arrangement for the successful launch and subsequent implementation of the scheme.

AP Bala Sanjeevani Scheme 2018 for Pregnant Women & Children
Under this scheme, only the residents of Andhra Pradesh will be eligible. This scheme is applicable to pregnant women and children under 6 years of age. High protein diet is to given to eligible people to ensure proper health to the people. The important features, objectives and highlights of Bala Sanjeevani Scheme 2018 are as follows:-

To reduce infant mortality rate.
To avoid birth defect / diseases in newborns.
Eradication of Malnutrition in the state.
To provide nutritious food to pregnant women during their pregnancy period.
