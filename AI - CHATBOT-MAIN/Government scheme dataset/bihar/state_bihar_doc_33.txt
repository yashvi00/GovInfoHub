
State government Bihar has announced a new housing scheme for the fourth grade government employees in its budget for the financial year 2017-18. The chief minister of Bihar presented budget 2017-18 with an expenditure of Rs. 14,555 crore for development of different sectors.
The government has also announced to implement a government employees housing scheme for the fourth-grade government employees and allocated Rs. 873 crores for the same.
Through this scheme, Bihar government will develop housing projects especially for fourth-grade government employees and offer them affordable house with all basic residential facilities.
