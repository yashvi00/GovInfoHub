Vehicle Scrapping Policy
Launched: 25 March 2018
Main Objective: Scrap 15 Year Old Vehicles
Under Vehicle Scrapping Policy, it is proposed to scrap 15 years or old vehicles. Govt. is keen to implement Voluntary Vehicle Fleet Modernization Programme (V-VMP) Policy to reduce pollution in the country. This will also give a boost to vehicle scrap business and thus to make it an automobile industry hub.
