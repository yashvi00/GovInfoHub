Shadi Shagun Scheme
Announced: 9 August 2017
Main Objective: Rs. 51000 to Muslim Girls
Under Shadi Shagun Scheme, govt. will provide Rs. 51000 as wedding gift to all theF girls belonging to Muslim Community. This amount would be applicable to all girls from minority sections who have completed graduation. This scheme will ensure that girls from backward sections must get necessary higher education before getting married.
