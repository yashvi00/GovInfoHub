
हरियाणा सरकार ने छोटे व्यापारियों और दुकानदारों के लिए मुख्यमंत्री व्यापारी क्षतिपूर्ति बीमा योजना और मुख्यमंत्री व्यापारी सामूहिक निजी दुर्घटना बीमा योजना की शुरुआत करी, जानिये क्या हैं इसके लाभ और पाएं पूरी जानकारीHaryana government has started Mukhyamantri Vyapari Kshatipurti Beema Yojana 2019. Under this scheme, govt. will provide an insurance cover ranging from Rs. 5 lakh to Rs. 25 lakh as per their stock / turnover. This coverage would be applicable to all registered small and medium traders, shopkeepers, self employed persons. CM Manohar Lal Khattar has also started Mukhyamantri Vyapari Samuhik Niji Durghatna Bima Yojana to provide Rs. 5 lakh insurance to traders.
The total premium of Rs. 38 crore for both Mukhyamantri Vyapari Samuhik Niji Durghatna Bima Yojana & Mukhyamantri Vyapari Kshatipurti Beema Yojana would be borne by the Haryana government. On this occasion, CM has also presented insurance certificates to six beneficiaries under this scheme.
CM has also made several other announcements for the welfare of different sections of society.

Haryana Mukhyamantri Vyapari Kshatipurti Bima Yojana
The important features and highlights of this scheme are as follows:-


Haryana CM Vyapari Kshatipurti Bima Yojna


Launch Date
11 September 2019


Who are its Beneficiaries
All registered small and medium traders, retailers, shopkeepers & self employed persons


Number of Beneficiaries
3.13 lakh


Cases for Applicability
Govt. will compensate loss of trader’s stock caused due to fire, theft, flood and earthquake and damage to furniture and other goods


Amount of Insurance
Rs. 5 Lakh to Rs. 25 Lakh


Turnover Limit
Rs. 5 lakh insurance for annual turnover upto Rs. 20 lakh, Rs. 10 lakh insurance coverage for turnover b/w Rs. 20 lakh to Rs. 50 lakh, Rs. 15 lakh insurance cover for turnover b/w Rs. 50 lakh to Rs. 1 crore, Insurance cover of Rs. 20 lakh for turnover b/w Rs. 1 crore to Rs. 1.5 crore, Rs. 25 lakh for traders having turnover of more than Rs. 1.5 crore.


An insurance cover of Rs. 5 lakh to Rs. 25 lakh would be provided to registered traders on the basis of their turnover.

The loss of stock due to any of the above mentioned reasons would attract compensation from the Haryana govt. Around 3.13 lakh registered small and medium traders would be benefited under this scheme.
