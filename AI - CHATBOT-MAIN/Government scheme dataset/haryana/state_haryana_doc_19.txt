
बिजली बचत के लिए 5 स्टार रेटिंग पंप सेट किसानों को लगाने के लिए हरियाणा सरकार की योजना की शुरुआतHaryana government is going to start a new 5 Star Rated Pump Set Scheme for farmers in the state. Under this scheme, govt. will provide 5 Star rated pumps of water to kisaan at nominal prices. These pumps would be efficient and will reduce the loss of energy i.e helps in energy conservation. Here we are providing you the details of this water pumpset scheme of the state government.
In order to realize the vision of Doubling of Farmers Income by 2022, the state govt. has come up with a new 5 Star Rated Water Pumpset Scheme. In this scheme, each farmer can get one pump set for installation in their farms / fields.

Haryana 5 Star Rated Pumps For Farmers to Save Energy
As we all know that water pump sets consume a large amount of energy to provide water to fields of farmers. So, the state govt. of Haryana has launched 5 star rated energy efficient pump set scheme for farmers. All the new tubewell applicants are herby informed that it is mandatory to install these 5 star rated pump sets.
Warranty & Details of 5 Star Rated Water Pump Set Scheme Haryana
The complete details of Haryana 5 Star Rated pumpset scheme are as follows:-

The state govt. of Haryana will start installation of 5 Star rated pumps in farmers fields to conserve energy.
Electricity Department of Haryana will procure these 5 star rated pumpsets for farmers.
All the farmers will get these 5 star rated pumps at nominal cost i.e the cost which they used to pay for earlier low energy conservation pumps.
All the pumps will come up with a free onsite guarantee / warranty period of 5 years.
