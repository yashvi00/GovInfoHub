Labour department of Haryana invites online application form for House Purchase / Construction Loan Scheme (मकान की खरीद/निर्माण हेतु ऋण) at hrylabour.gov.in. All the registered building and construction workers (BOCW) can avail this assistance for 1 time during their lifetime. Haryana Labour Welfare Fund House Purchase / Construction Loan Scheme, laborers can avail assistance to purchase new homes or re-construct or renovate existing houses. 
All the BOCW laborers can now apply online for Haryana Labour Welfare Board House Construction / Purchase Interest Free Loan Scheme and fill online registration / application form.

Haryana Labour House Purchase / Construction Loan Scheme 2019
Haryana Labour Welfare Fund Death Assistance Scheme 2019 aims to provide loans upto Rs. 2 lakh to BOCW laborers for purchase / construction of houses. This Interest Free Home Loan scheme by Haryana Labour Department will enable labourers to renovate / purchase their own houses.
Haryana Labour Welfare Fund House Construction / Purchase Loan Scheme Form Online
All the candidates can firstly visit the official website at hrylabour.gov.in. At the homepage, go to the “E-Services” section and make registration using Aadhaar card. Then make a login at the homepage of the official Labour dept. website and fill the Haryana Labour Welfare Fund House Purchase / Construction Loan Scheme online registration form.

Haryana Labour Welfare Board House Purchase / Construction Loan Scheme Eligibility
Under the House Purchase / Construction Loan Scheme of Haryana Labour Welfare Board, labour dept. provides interest free loans upto Rs. 2 lakh amount to registered labourers. The eligibility criteria to avail Haryana Labour House Purchase / Construction Loan Scheme are as follows:-

Haryana Labour Department House Purchase / Construction Loan Scheme Conditions
People can follow the below mentioned conditions to avail financial aid under Labour Welfare Board House Purchase / Construction Loan Scheme in Haryana:-

All the registered labourers must have minimum 5 years of membership / subscription.
The age of registered labourers must be at-least 8 years less than 60 years.
The maximum age of construction workers must be 52 years so that he / she can repay loan amount in the next 8 years.
This facility will remain applicable for 1 time in a lifetime.

Labour Welfare Fund House Purchase / Construction Loan Scheme 2019 is available for all registered workers of the Haryana state.
