
Haryana Police has launched a new mobile application namely Durga Shakti App for women. This app will enable any women or girl in distress to seek immediate help of the Police in case of any emergency. Now all women using smartphones can make Durga Shakti App Download through Google Play Store and access alert & other features of app. 
Any woman / girl can register herself after downloading the Durga Shakti App through Google Playstore on their mobile phones. Alert button feature is available which will enable the state police department to track the location of girl in case of emergency.
This app is very useful as we can’t help our family girl every time but now we can help our women, thanks to this Durga Shakti mobile app of Haryana police.
