
Haryana CM Manohar Lal Khattar announces to provide grant to Industries Providing Jobs to 50% & more haryanavi youths. Now all the C & D category enterprises will get grant of Rs. 3,000 per month for each employee for the upcoming 3 years. This is to increase the enrollment / employment of local youths in these industries. In Haryana, the state govt. is also planning to provide a similar type of grant to all A & B block industries. 
CM Manohar Lal has made announcement while attending a conclave event as Chief Guest in New Delhi. This is major initiative to boost the industrial development. For this, Haryana govt. is paying Rs. 2 / unit in total electricity bills of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSME).
Auto, Textiles and Pharmaceuticals sectors in Haryana have been developing with a fast rate.
