Department of Social Justice & Empowerment, Haryana Govt. is implementing Disability Pension Scheme for Diyang Jans. Under this Viklang Pension Yojana 2020, all the disabled persons of Haryana domicile with more than 60% disability and above 18 years of age will get Rs. 1800 as monthly pension. People of Haryana residing in the state for the past 3 years can now Download Handicapped Pension Form in PDF Format. Moreover, specially abled people can also check Disability Pension Status and Haryana Pension Beneficiary List at socialjusticehry.gov.in 
Haryana Viklang Pension Yojana and other Social Security Schemes like Old Age Pension Scheme, Widow Pension Scheme are fully implemented by the state government. The new Haryana Pension Rules are in effect from 1 November 2017 in which govt. has fixed the rate of allowance at Rs. 1800. The total self income from all sources must not exceed the minimum wages of unskilled labour as notified by the Labor Department. 
People can download the Handicapped Pension Scheme 2020 Application Form in PDF Format and fill it to avail the benefits of Disability Pension Scheme. People can also check the Handicapped Pension Status and Viklang Pension Yojana List of Beneficiaries.

Haryana Disability Pension Scheme 2020 Details
Here is an overview of the Haryana Disability Pension Scheme 2020:- 
Eligibility Criteria

Age 18 years or above (Male/Female)
Domicile of Haryana and residing in Haryana for last 3 years at time of submission of application.
His/her close relative such as parents son, son’s, sons are not supporting him and his/her own annual income from all sources is not more than minimum wages of unskilled Labour as notified by Labour Department and circulated by department on year to year basis.
Disability 60% and above

Blindness
Low Vision
Leprosy Cured
Hearing Impairment
Locomotors Disability
Mental Retardation with I.Q. not exceeding 50
Mental Illness

Benefits Provided
Rs. 1800/- per month (Rs. 2000/- w.e.f 1.11.2018 payable in Dec, 2018)

Documents Required

Disability certificate of 60% & above.
Birth Certificate
Aadhaar Card
Residential Proof : Any one of the following documents for the domicile of Haryana issued prior to 15 years to date of application will be accepted :-

Ration Card
Voter Card
Name of applicant in Voter List
Pan Card
Driving License
Passport
Electricity bill/ Water bill
Documents of House and land
Copy of LIC Policy
Registered Rent Deed of House
Permanent resident certificate of Haryana



Fees for the Service
Govt. Charges is Rs. 0, Kendra Service Charges is Rs. 10, Atal Seva Kendra (CSC) Service Charges is Rs. 30. The RTS Time Limit is 60 Days.
Haryana Viklang Pension Yojana 2020 Application Form
All the candidates who wants to apply for the Disability Pension Scheme can now download the Handicapped Pension Scheme Registration Form. To download the Haryana Viklang Pension Yojana 2020 Application Form, candidates can follow the steps given below:-

Visit the official website of Dept. of Social Justice & Empowerment socialjusticehry.gov.in
On the homepage, click at “Forms” tab present in the main menu or directly click this link
Direct Link to Download Application Form for Disability Pension PDF – Click Here
The Haryana Viklang Pension Yojana 2020 Application Form will appear as follows:-
Haryana Handicapped Pension Form
All the candidates will have to fill the Haryana Viklang Yojana Application Form and then submit the completed application to Social Welfare Officer in District / Taluka in Haryana.

On verification and consequent approval from the issuing authority, applicants will start receiving the pension amount directly into their bank account. Applicants can check their Disability Pension Status and name in the Haryana Handicapped Pension List subject to the Eligibility Criteria. This scheme will enable all the disabled of 18 years of age or above with 60% or more disability to get Rs. 1800 monthly pension.
Haryana Viklang Yojana Status / Track Handicapped Pension Beneficiary Details
Candidates can now check the Handicapped Pension Status and also Track Viklang Pension Beneficiary Details as follows:-

Visit the same official website socialjusticehry.gov.in
On the homepage of Social Justice Dept., click at “आधार/पैंशन आई.डी./खाता संख्या से पैंशन विवरण देखें” or directly click this link
Then a new window with “लाभार्थी के पेंशन विवरण देखें / Track Beneficiary Pension Details” heading will open. Representative Image is shown in the figure:-
Track Viklang Yojana Beneficiary Details
Here candidates can Track Handicapped Pension Beneficiaries Details through पेंशन आईडी / Pension ID, खाता संख्या / Account Number (with IFSC Code) or आधार संख्या / Aadhaar Number and Security Code”.
Finally candidates can click at “विवरण देखें / View Details” button to display the Haryana Pension Beneficiary Status.

Registered beneficiaries can also check their Name in Haryana Pension Beneficiary List 2020. 
Haryana Handicapped Pension List 2020
Registered pensioners can check whether their name appears Haryana Pension List of Beneficiaries 2020 or not. People can now Check Handicapped Pension Scheme Beneficiary List Online as follows:-

Visit the official website socialjusticehry.gov.in
At homepage, click at “लाभार्थियों की सूची देखें / View List of Beneficiaries” link or directly click this link
In the new window, Haryana Widow Pension Scheme List of Beneficiaries 2020 will appear as follows:-
Haryana Pension Beneficiary List
Now enter your जिला / District, क्षेत्र / Area, खण्ड / नगरपालिका / Block / Municipality, गाँव / वार्ड / सेक्टर / Village / Ward / Sector, पैंशन का नाम / Pension Type, छांटने का क्रम / Sort Order and click at “लाभार्थी सूची देखें / View Beneficiary List“.
Afterwards the complete pension list with pensioners Name, Gender, Age, Enrolment date, Pension Amount, Ward / Sector, Mohalla / Colony, Aadhaar Number, Bank / PO Name, Account Number and Account Uploading Date will get displayed on the screen as follows:-
Haryana Handicapped Pension List
Finally, candidates can hit “Ctrl+F” to find your name in the Divyang Pension List of Beneficiaries.

For more details, candidates can visit the official website http://socialjusticehry.gov.in/
