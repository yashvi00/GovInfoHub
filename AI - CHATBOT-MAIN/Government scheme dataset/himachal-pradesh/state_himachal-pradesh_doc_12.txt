HP Free School Uniform Scheme – Atal School Vardi Yojana 2020Progress of Atal Vardi Yojna Till August 2020 Other Decisions Taken in Cabinet Meeting in Himachal Pradesh
Himachal Pradesh govt. has launched Atal School Vardi Yojana for the students in all the state run schools. Under this Free Uniform Scheme, all the students of class 1st to 12th will get these free uniforms. Students are provided with free school uniforms for the FY 2018-19, 2019-20 & 2020-21. In Atal Vardi Yojna in 2018-19, around 8,30,945 students of class 1-12 had received 2 sets of free school uniforms with an outlay of Rs. 73.5 crore. 
Under the Atal Vardi Yojana, students of 1st, 6th and 9th class are also being provided with school bags in addition to two uniforms in a year. Till 26 August 2020, around 2,56,514 students of class 1, 6 & 9 had also received school bags with an estimated expenditure of Rs. 7.84 crore. 
This scheme would go a long way in inculcating confidence among the children from poor families. The Free Uniform Scheme was named after former PM Atal Bihari Vajpayee because of his special love for the hill folk.

HP Free School Uniform Scheme – Atal School Vardi Yojana 2020
Himachal Pradesh govt. had already started procurement, supply and distribution of school uniforms to the school students of class 1st to 12th under the Atal School Vardi Yojana. These uniforms are being given to the students in state run schools for the year 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21 through the Himachal Pradesh State Civil Supplies Corporation.
Progress of Atal Vardi Yojna Till August 2020 
As mentioned above, around 8.3 lakh students of class 1-12 had already received 2 sets of free school uniforms worth Rs. 73.5 crore in 2018-19. Moreover, around 2.5 lakh students of class 1, 6, 9 had already received bags worth Rs. 7.84 crore. The decision to implement HP Atal Vardi Yojna was taken in the cabinet meeting held at Dharamshala chaired by CM Jai Ram Thakur. The free uniform scheme aims to bring about uniformity among students in the classrooms by providing similar uniform to all irrespective of their economic status.

Other Decisions Taken in Cabinet Meeting in Himachal Pradesh
In the cabinet committee meeting held in 2018-19, govt. had also approved to allocate execution of 100 MW Sorang hydro electric project in Kinnaur to M/s Greenko East Coast Power Projects Pvt. Ltd. for execution. This was done by allowing transfer of 100% equity shares in Himachal Sorang Power Private Limited in favour of Greenko Group Company namely Greenko East Coast Power Projects Pvt. Ltd. Along with the Atal School Vardi Yojana, these decisions were taken for the overall development of the Himachal Pradesh state.
