
Himachal Pradesh CM Jai Ram Thakur has launched HP MyGov Portal at himachal.mygov.in. Now people can make HP My Gov Portal Registration / Login 2020 at the official website. This portal will go a long way in strengthening public participation in governance process. Himachal My Gov Portal is an innovative platform to build partnership b/w citizens and government with usage of technology for growth and development of India. गतिविधियाँ – समूह, कार्यों, चर्चाओं, जनमत, ब्लॉग और विशेष चर्चाओं मे भाग लेकर राष्ट्र निर्माण का हिस्सा बनें और इसमें अपना योगदान दें!
The My Gov Himachal Portal will enable people to communicate with govt. by providing their views, suggestions, feedback and discontentment. HP state govt. will take appropriate measures on constructive criticism and will synergize all contributions for betterment of Himachal Pradesh.
The salient features of Himachal My Gov Portal are discussions, polls, tasks, talks and blogs on several groups based on diverse governance and public policy issues.

HP My Gov Portal Registration / Login at himachal.mygov.in
Here is the complete process to make registration and login at the Himachal Pradesh My Gov Portal:-

Firstly visit the official website at himachal.mygov.in
At the homepage, click at the “पंजीकरण करें” link in the main menu to open the HP My Gov Portal Registration Form as shown below:-
Himachal My Gov Portal Registration Form
Here candidates can make registration using their social media profile as on facebook, google, twitter, linkedin or can enter their details to create a new account.

All the new users can then make login and the login page will appear as shown below:-
Himachal MyGov Portal Login Form
It is important to note that users with @ gov.in or @ nic.in ID can directly login with their e-mail Id and passowrd.

After making login, all the candidates can participate in various activities at the portal (as mentioned below) and communicate with the HP government. Previously, the state govt. had already launched HP Mukhyamantri Seva Sankalp Helpline Number to establish 2 way communication between citizens and government.

Activities at Himachal My Gov Portal

समूह – आपकी रूचि के विषय
कार्य – ऑनलाइन और बाहरी कार्य करें
चर्चा – समूह और चर्चा में भाग लें
जनमत – जनमत का हिस्सा बनें
ब्लॉग – मेरी सरकार की नवीनतम गतिविधयों के बारे में पढ़ें
वार्ता – सुशासन पर हुई विशेष चर्चा सुनें

To directly connect to chief minister of Himachal Pradesh, a new CM App is also launched.
