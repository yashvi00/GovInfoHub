
Government of Himachal Pradesh has launched a new loan scheme for all the Himachali students. The government has announced the new scheme named as Mukhya Mantri Gyandeep Yojana in the financial budget 2016-17.
Under the Mukhya Mantri Gyandeep Yojana, the state government will provide interest subvention of 4 percent to all Himachali Students availing education loan upto Rs 10 lakh.
The state government has launched the new loan scheme in order to encourage deserving and meritorious students pursuing professional/technical and higher education courses in recognised institution in the country.
