
Himachal Pradesh Government has announced Mukhya Mantri Vardi Yojana for students of 10+2 class. Under the Mukhya Mantri Vardi Yojana, the students of 10+2 will be provided uniform by the state government.
The scheme is expected to benefit 1.34 lakh students across the state. Uniform to 10+1 students will also be provided.
Mukhya Mantri Vardi Yojana is one of the several new initiatives announced by the Government of Himachal Pradesh in 2016-17 financial budget.
