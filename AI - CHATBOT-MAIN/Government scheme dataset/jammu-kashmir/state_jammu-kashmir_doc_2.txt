Jammu and Kashmir Voter List 2020 (JK CEO Electoral Rolls) Download @ ceojk.nic.in
Below is the complete procedure to download JKCEO Voter List 2020 (PDF Electoral Rolls):-

Firstly visit the official JK CEO website at ceojk.nic.in
At the homepage, click at the “Download Electoral Rolls” tab present on the right side of the page:-
JK CEO Voter List 2020
The direct link to open the voters list of J&K is Download JK CEO Voter List. After clicking this link, people can download Assembly Constituency wise report or Polling Station wise report as shown below:-
JKCEO Voter List 2020 Download
On this page, candidates can click at the “PS Wise Report” option, select the language and then click the “Show” button to open the Polling Station Wise Report as shown below:-
ceojk.nic.in Voter List PS Wise
Here candidates can select the district, assembly constituency, polling station and then click at “Get Report” button. Finally, click at the ‘File link’ to download the PDF of CEO Voter List 2020 Jammu and Kashmir as shown below:-
Voter List 2020 Jammu and Kashmir

Candidates can find their name manually in the downloaded CEO Voters List of Jammu & Kashmir.

Find Name in JKCEO Voter List – JK Voter ID Card Online
To check the name in the JKCEO Voter List, visit the official website at ceojammukashmir.nic.in. At the homepage, click at the “Search Your Name” tab to open the name find page as shown below:-
Voter ID Card Online JK
Candidates can search their name in the CEO Voter List 2020 with Photo and download the JK Voter ID Card Online.If the name of the person does not appears in the JKCEO Voters List, then citizens can apply online for voter card Jammu & Kashmir. In case of any query, candidates can call on the helpline number 1950. 
For more details, visit the official website http://ceojammukashmir.nic.in/ or http://ceojk.nic.in/
