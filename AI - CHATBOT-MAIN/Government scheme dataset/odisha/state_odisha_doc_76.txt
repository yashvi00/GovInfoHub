
The state government of Odisha is going to launch Odia Bhasha Brutti Puraskar across the state. This is a scholarship program under which scholarship will be given to the students having good skills in Odia language. This scholarship program was announced by the CM of Odisha on the eve of Children’s Day (14 November 2017).
Under Odia Bhasha Brutti Puraskar, the student who score highest marks in Odia language test would get Rs. 5,000 cash reward by the state government. The 10 students will be selected in each block in the state who will get the cash reward of Rs. 5000.
Odia Bhasha Brutti Puraskar
However, this scheme is only for the students of Class 10th. This scholarship program for meritorious students will be implemented at the block level. The process to apply to avail the scholarship will soon be available with complete procedure.

The special arrangement was made on the occasion of 2017 children’s day at state level. This event was organized in the Capital High School Ground in Bhubaneswar. That time about 4,500 students from 24 schools listening attended the programme. Special arrangements were also made at the block level primary schools to live telecast the speech of Odisha CM.
On the occasion of children’s day, CM announced few other schemes including Mukhyamantri Medhabruti Puraskar and Mo School Campaign for the students, teachers, and parents.
