Samajik Suraksha Pension Yojana in Rajasthan is a social security pension scheme under which several pension schemes are merged. The interested eligible candidates can apply for Samajik Suraksha Pension Yojana by downloading the application form from the official website at rajssp.raj.nic.in.

Under the Samajik Suraksha Pension Yojana, the interested applicant can apply for pension under any of the following three heads
– Old age
– Widows and Parityakta
– Disabled persons
The dully filled application form can be submitted at district sub divisional or block development office. After receiving the application, the details would be verified by Tehsildar and sanction would be confirmed by district sub divisional or block development office and sanction order will go to the treasury or sub-treasury office for disbursement of pension to the beneficiary.
Apply Online through e-mitra
Online applications for Samajik Suraksha Pension Yojana can also be filled through the E-Mitra kendra spread across the state.
