
Deendayal Rasoi Yojana is a new food scheme announced by Rajasthan government in Ujjain. The purpose of the scheme is to provide quality meal to the poor people who are unable to afford food at normal market rates.
Deendayal Rasoi Yojana
Deendayal Rasoi Yojana will provide a full meal to poor people, workers and their families. Under the scheme, nutritious meal would be provided to the poor in the city at just Rs. 5/-. To implement the scheme in the city, bhojnalayas will be opened at different locations including, Dewasgate, Nanakheda, Railway Station, Chimanganj Krishi Upaj Mandi and Siddhwat.
The scheme will be activated on 1 April 2017 and the announcement of Rajasthan government Deendayal Rasoi Yojana in Ujjain was made in the meeting by the government.
