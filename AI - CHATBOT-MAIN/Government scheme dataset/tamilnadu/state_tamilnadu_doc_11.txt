
Tamilnadu TNSand Vehicle List 2019 can be checked online at the official website at www.tnsand.in. Tamil Nadu government has now released the TN Sand Quarry Vehicle List through the official TNSAND Portal at www.tnsand.in. This vehicle list of sand quarry in Tamilnadu can even be checked through the TNSAND android app. 
Other than checking the Tamil Nadu Sand Guest Vehicle List, people can check TNSAND sand booking status, register new vehicle, make a new booking for TN sand, move the order, request a refund, check the status of their payment and refund check the complete list of registered vehicles.
Below are the complete steps to check the online TNSAND Beneficiary Vehicle List at www.tnsand.in.

Tamilnadu Sand Quarry Vehicle List 2019 at tnsand.in

Step 1 – Visit the official TNSAND website at www.tnsand.in
Step 2 – Then on the right side of the homepage, click at the “General People (பொது மக்கள்)” tab to open the tnsand booking page as shown below:-
TN Sand Quarry Vehicle List 2019
Step 3 – Then click at the “Vehicle List” tab on the left side as shown above. To check the Tamil Nadu Sand Guest Vehicle List, people can also directly click the link – https://www.tnsand.in/Guest/VehicleList to open the page as shown below:-
Vehicle List of Sand Quarry in Tamilnadu
Step 4 – Here candidates can select the name of the district as shown below:-
TNSAND Beneficiary Vehicle List 2019
Step 5 – Finally, candidates can click at the “Get PDF” button to open the Tamil Nadu Sand Guest Vehicle List PDF
Tamil Nadu Sand Guest Vehicle List

This list includes the name of the district, name, registration number of vehicles, mobile number and pincode. Now people are not required to wait in long lines for sand. Rather people can simply make vehicle registration and check their name in the TNSAND Vehicle List 2019 at the official website at www.tnsand.in.
