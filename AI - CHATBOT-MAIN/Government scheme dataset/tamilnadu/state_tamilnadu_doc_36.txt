மின்னணு அட்டைக்கான விண்ணப்பத்தின் நிலை
The status of your smart ration card application can be checked online by visiting the official website of Tamilnadu Public Distribution System at tnpds.gov.in. To know the application status, you need to enter the reference number which was sent to the mobile number of user while registration.

Step by step procedure to check the status of smart ration card application

Visit the official website of TNPDS at tnpds.gov.in
Click on ” மின்னணு அட்டை விண்ணப்பத்தின் நிலை” link in the right sidebar of the website homepage or if you wish to use English language version, choose the “English” language from top right part of the website and click “Smart Card Application Status” link.
On the next page, you will be shown a textbox field to enter your reference number, enter your ref. number in the textbox and click “Submit” button.
Once you click the “Submit” button, the status of your smart card application would be shown to you.
