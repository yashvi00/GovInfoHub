
Chief Minister K Chandrasekhar Rao government’s Double Bedroom Housing Scheme to be ready by March 2020. Telangana housing dept. announces that the two BHK dream housing project will soon come into realization as desired by CM KCR. 2 BHK Housing scheme is a flagship scheme of Telangana government to provide affordable 2BHK houses to eligible beneficiaries in the state.
Telangana govt. flagship programme of 2BHK housing scheme at Kollur in the city outskirts would be completed by March 2020. There are around 15,660 double bedroom flats which are being built in 125 acres of land. 
Nearly one lakh people will reside at this place after the completion of Double Bedroom (2 BHK) Housing Scheme project.

Telangana Double Bedroom (2BHK) Housing Scheme 2019-2020
Each house to be constructed under the Double Bedroom (2 BHK) Housing Scheme consists of two bedrooms, a hall, a kitchen and two toilets. The total built up area of each house is 560 sq.ft. and the cost of housing per each Dwelling Unit (DU) is Rs. 7.90 lakh. The cost of infrastructure per dwelling unit is Rs. 75,000. 
This is one of the biggest and most prestigious housing projects which would be taken up in Asia. These houses under 2 BHK housing scheme will provide all amenities and infrastructure like water, power, drainage, roads, shopping malls. In addition to this, parks, nursing homes, school, library, police out-post, fire station, bus station and parking facility in each block would be present.

The pattern of the housing units being constructed are in G+1 to G+9 pattern on the basis of land type at site of construction. The total project cost including housing plus infrastructure is around Rs 1,354.59 crore.

Chitra Ramachandran who is the Additional Chief Secretary for Housing in Telangana Housing Department has inspected the progress of Double Bedroom Housing Scheme. This 2BHK housing scheme is dream project which would come into realization as desired by the CM KCR. All the people belonging to below poverty line (BPL) category would be the beneficiaries of 2 BHK housing scheme.
