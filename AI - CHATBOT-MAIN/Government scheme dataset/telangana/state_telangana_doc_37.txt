
Telangana state government has launched a new programme for the empowerment of drivers in the state. Under the scheme, SC/ST/BC and minority drivers would be provided skill enhancement training, assistance for placement and financial help for the purchase of vehicle. The state government through the scheme aims to empower the drivers belonging to SC/ST/BC and minority categories.
Online applications at tsobmms.cgg.gov.in
The state government is inviting online applications for the scheme through its official website at tsobmms.cgg.gov.in. The interested candidates can apply for the scheme anytime between 15th to 28th February 2017.
To apply online for driver enhancement programme, the candidates should have minimum qualification of class VIII (8th) pass. The annual household income of the candidate should not exceed Rs. 1.5 Lakh in rural areas and Rs. 2 Lakh in urban areas. The applicants who have appeared in SSC exams (Pass or Fail) will be given preferences under the scheme.
