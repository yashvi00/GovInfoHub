2 BHK Housing scheme is a flagship scheme of Telangana Government to provide affordable 2BHK houses to eligible beneficiaries in the state. The state government has already completed the construction of about 80,000 homes in the financial year 2015-16 under the 2BHK housing scheme. In the year 2016-17, the state government has set a target of building 1.4 Crore 2BHK homes under the scheme.
Telangana 2BHK Housing Scheme Application Forms
Each house being constructed under the 2BHK housing scheme will consist of 2 Bedrooms, 1 Hall, One Kitchen and Two toilets with a plinth area of 560 Sq.ft. The estimated cost of the house is Rs. 5.30 Lakh in urban area excluding the infrastructure cost. The pattern of the housing units construction would be G+1 to G+9 based upon the type of land at site of construction.
Telangana 2BHK housing scheme application forms are now available at MeeSeva centers across the states. The interested eligible candidates can submit the filled application forms at any of their nearest MeeSeva centers.

How to Apply for 2BHK housing scheme in Telangana
The interest applicants just need to visit their MeeSeva centers and submit the filled application form along with the copies of the required documents. The applicants need to fill in their personal details, family details, income, aadhar number, address, contact details and some others in the application form along with a passport size photograph.

Allotment of houses under the 2BHK housing scheme will be done on the lottery draw basis. The draw will be held among the eligible candidates for a particular category of house in any particular locality. Out of the total houses being constructed, 60% of the Houses are earmarked for Outright sale and 40% of houses for hire purchase.
Eligibility Criteria for 2BHK Housing Scheme
No application shall be considered if the applicant:

Owns a house any where in the Urban areas and Urban agglomerations in the State of Telangana.
The income does not fall within the income ceiling limits fixed by A.P.Housing Board from time to time for the different categories.
The income is the aggregate personal income regularly derived by the applicant at the time of application.
Lots are drawn in case the numbers of applications received are more than number of houses notified.
RESERVATIONS: Quota of reservations is followed in the matter of allotment of houses.
